Pre-Flight Medical

Twelve weeks of preparation so your body disagrees with space a little less violently than it otherwise would.

The clearance program

Every passenger completes screening at one of eight TELETREKS clinics (Cape Canaveral, Baikonur, Tanegashima, Kourou, Mumbai, Nairobi, Sydney, Reykjavik). The program includes bone-density baselines, cardiovascular stress tests under simulated g-loads, radiation education, and zero-gravity acclimatization.

What we screen for

  • Undiagnosed cardiac arrhythmia or untreated hypertension
  • Severe osteoporosis or recent fractures incompatible with landing loads
  • Uncontrolled psychiatric conditions that impair judgment under stress
  • Medications that interact with cryo-preservatives (full list provided at intake)

Cryo-specific

Elective cryo-sleep requires additional vascular imaging and metabolic panels. If you opt for awake transit, you will still complete baseline labs — different risks, same rigor.

Privacy

Medical records are encrypted, mission-need only, and never sold. You may request deletion of Earth-side copies after successful arrival, subject to regulatory retention rules.

Questions? Contact Mission Control and ask for Medical Services.
